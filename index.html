About Us

RAR Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-performance industrial adsorbents.

Founded on the principles of engineering excellence, we provide critical solutions for refineries, petrochemical plants, and gas processing units worldwide.

Our focus is on maximizing process efficiency and equipment lifespan through advanced materials like Activated Alumina, specialized Chlorine Guards, and high-purity Catalyst Carriers.

Specialized Solutions

Activated Alumina

Superior moisture and impurity adsorption with high surface area and mechanical strength.

Chlorine Guards

Catalyst Carriers

High-purity support materials providing thermal stability and structural integrity.
