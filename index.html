About Us

RAR Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-performance industrial adsorbents.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex chemical engineering and practical industrial application. We specialize in Activated Alumina, Chlorine Guards, and Catalyst Carriers for global petrochemical industries, ensuring maximum process efficiency and equipment protection.

Our Specialized Products

Activated Alumina

Highly porous form of aluminum oxide with a massive surface area for superior moisture and impurity adsorption.

Chlorine Guards

Specialized protective agents designed to neutralize acidic chlorides and prevent downstream corrosion in refineries.

Catalyst Carriers

High-purity support materials engineered to provide the structural skeleton and thermal stability for active catalysts.
